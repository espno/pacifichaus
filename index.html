What to Expect

I've picked a service.
Now what?

Here's what working together actually looks like — and what it doesn't. No surprises, no overpromising. Just honest expectations from the start.

What I take care of

Once we've agreed on scope, I handle everything: we'll collaborate on strategy and find the best way to go about your vision — then content direction, captions, scheduling, community management, reporting, and KOL coordination. Price does not include shoots or third-party consultants. You'll have a Notion client portal where everything is visible — calendar, approvals queue, reports, and brand assets. You approve content. That's your main job.

What I need from you

Access to your accounts, your brand assets, and context about your business. The best results come from authentic content — your space, your team, your product. I'll brief you on exactly what to capture. The more real material you give me, the stronger the work.

Communication

I work AEST/AEDT aligned hours. You'll hear from me proactively — not the other way around. No chasing for updates. Monthly reports land in your portal on time. Urgent things get handled same day. Check-in calls are scheduled in advance.

Timeline for results

Engagement shifts start showing in weeks 4–6. Meaningful brand awareness and follower growth happen between weeks 8–12. This is why I work on a 3-month minimum — it's the realistic timeline for results that actually move your business.

On the subject of "going viral"

Viral is not always the right KPI for your brand. A post that reaches 500,000 people who will never walk into your venue is worth less than one that reaches 5,000 people who are actively looking for exactly what you offer. The goal is to reach more of your target consumers — slowly, steadily, and with quality. Better reach means better leads. Better leads mean real customers. That's what we're building toward.
